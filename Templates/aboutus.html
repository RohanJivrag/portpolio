    Welcome to Coding Point Classes


    Our Story

    Founded in 2015 by Om and Sanket, Coding Point Classes started as a small initiative to help local students learn the basics of programming. Over the years, we have grown into a well-respected institution, offering a wide range of courses from beginner to advanced levels.

    What We Offer

    We provide comprehensive courses in web development, data science, AI, and more. Our expert instructors and flexible learning options ensure that you get the best education possible.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to create a world where everyone has the opportunity to learn and succeed in the field of technology. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and supportive learning environment.
